About Us

Our Coffee Leaf Disease Detection System leverages Artificial Intelligence to help farmers identify and classify coffee leaf diseases such as Leaf Rust and Phoma early. This empowers farmers to take timely preventive measures, reducing crop losses and improving yields.

The project aims to support sustainable coffee farming in Uganda and promote digital agriculture solutions.

Developed by Joseph Walusimbi and Chelangat Specioza passionate data analysts and AI enthusiasts focused on applying machine learning to solve real-world challenges in agriculture and climate.
